The Keyes Company is a luxury real estate brokerage in Florida, known for its long history, local expertise, and comprehensive services. The company prides itself on its deep understanding of the communities it serves and its dedication to client satisfaction

My role as a Graphic Designer for The Keyes Company is to create designs whilst incorporating their branding for the design. Creating designs such as postcards, brochures, flyers, open house signages, email headers & footers and other design that they require.
