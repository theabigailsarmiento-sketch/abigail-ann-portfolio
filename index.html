FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What types of clients do you work with?

I work with diverse clients across various industries, both locally and abroad. I’ve collaborated with businesses and individuals from the US, Australia, and my home country, delivering motion graphics and video editing solutions tailored to their unique goals.

What is your design style?

My style adapts to each project—I combine sleek, professional visuals with creative storytelling. Whether it’s bold and energetic or minimal and elegant, I tailor my work to fit the brand and audience.

What is your experience in graphic design?

I’ve built my experience in graphic design by combining creativity with technical skills—crafting motion graphics, animations, and video edits that communicate ideas clearly and memorably. Working with diverse clients locally and internationally has strengthened my ability to adapt to different industries and styles.

How do I get started on a project with you?

Getting started is super simple! Just share your project idea or the kind of video you’re looking for, and I’ll help you shape it into a clear plan. From there, we’ll talk about your goals, timeline, and style preferences so I can bring your vision to life. Once we’re aligned, I’ll get to work and keep you updated every step of the way!
